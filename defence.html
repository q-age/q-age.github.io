Quantum Defence Technologies

A new age of defence

Alongside the all peaceful uses of quantum computing, there are defensive uses too. The need is arguable in the sense that we may not be alone in this universe, and therefore there are potential threats. It might therefore be considered wise to be prepared for such threats, as a planetary system. If 'alien' threats do exist, their offensive capability may be vastly superior to our defences.

    Under considered investigation:

  • Ability to absorb or teleport energy from direct energy (DE) weapons or projectiles away from target.
  • Ability to neutralise an offensive weapon from a distance by teleporting material away from that weapon, disabling it.
  • Use and generation of defensive 'shields' such as plasmas that may act as a barrier for DE weapons and projectiles.
  • Ability to disable energy systems on and within offensive platforms.
