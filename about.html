About Q-Age

A charitable company

Q-Age was formed upon the idea of quantum machine learning while studying at the University of Liverpool. Our founder decided that this was the goal, and would also form the fundamential underpinning for other technologies.

The intent of benefitiing society has always been at the heart of technological research, but Q-Age understands that sometimes, preparedness for hostility is sometimes required. Our ethical values require us to be transparent, sustainable, and maintain integrity at all times. Some of the technologies we hope to yield from our research and development have immense destructive capability, so although we wish to remain transparent about what we hope to do, and what we can do, we will not find it ethical to publish how we do it. We also wish to encourage the formation of a planetary group of experts that maintain personal high moral values, and in a transparent manner, are responsible and accountable to civilisation on Earth. Much like governments today, we wish to help form an international government based on ethical values that has the respect of national governments, gained by the carefully supervised and deliberate distribution of advanced automated technologies (AATs). AATs will form the currency that will accelerate ethical progress within nations and assist bring about global changes such as removing borders, ensuring that all persons are housed, have food, clean water, sewerage, education and entertainment that communities require to function peacefully.

We wish to bring Earth about to form a utopia for all.
